Welcome to Our Kindergarten!

At Sunshine Kindergarten, we create a joyful and supportive environment where children can learn and grow. Our engaging curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, creativity, and social development. We aim to spark curiosity and a love for learning in every child, preparing them for their educational journey ahead.

Weekly Schedule

Day Activity Description
Monday Creative Arts & Crafts Children explore their creativity through painting, drawing, and crafting.
Tuesday Interactive Storytelling Engaging stories that encourage imagination and vocabulary building.
Wednesday Nature Exploration Outdoor activities focusing on the environment and nature awareness.
Thursday STEM Activities Hands-on experiments and projects to inspire curiosity in science and technology.
Friday Music & Movement Fun music sessions and dance to promote physical activity and rhythm.
